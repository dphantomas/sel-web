**Sanacion en Luz** began to arrive to us in August 2020. The pandemic was passing and in a moment of connection Sananda and Lady Nada came to us. They started giving us information about how Free Will works and how we are using our free will as humans. They showed us that we were Divine Complements, that we have long been working accompanying different processes of humanity together with Gaya and that at this time it is necessary that we receive their guidance for the next process, Sanacion en Luz.

_«Sanacion en Luz has begun. This process begins to be Sanacion en Luz.»_

They explained that we will do this work and that we will be guided from Divine Complement to Divine Complement and that we will have meetings with them every two days. The Council of Twelve was ever present at these meetings, holding, creating what they called the Capsule.

It was explained to us that this process was different from what was already known, different from what was already done, which was specific to the era that was about to begin, the Age of Light. They would introduce us to new concepts that would help us understand a phrase that they told us at that time and that is being resignified as we move forward in the process of Sanacion en Luz:

**_«Nothing you have done so far defines who you Are.»_**

They showed us the different levels of Free Will and how we justify our choices, also when we know they will not lead us on a good path.

_«With each of those levels of Free Will there is a self that corresponds to each level. It seems obvious, but it is not, because, who observes? Who is watching what is happening? Which self is watching what is happening?»_

The first stage in Sanacion en Luz is about Observing our moments of low vibration, thoughts, feelings, emotions, reactions and so on.  
In each of these events there is a _self_ guiding us. That _lower self_ is so integrated into our system that we define ourselves in that way, with those reactions, with those ways of thinking. For example, when we say something like «I was angry, but how can I not be angry at such a situation» we are justifying choosing that _lower self_. At that moment we integrated it and we identified with it.

It is important to **take power away** from the _lower self_ so that it stops guiding our choices.  
It is important to **stop believing** that we are that _lower self_.  
It is important to **stop identifying** with that _lower self_.  
It is important to **Recognize who we are**, beyond that _lower self_.
