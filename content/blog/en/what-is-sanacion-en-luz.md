The New Human walks in **Purity**, **Expansion**, **Absolute-Presence**, all concepts that take on new meaning in the Age of Light. The New Human already walks on Gaya. The New Human walks aware of his **Original Plan** and lives in full **Recognition**.

**Sanacion en Luz** comes to us for the **Age of Light**.  
**Sanacion en Luz** is a process designed for evolution into the **New Human**.  
**Sanacion en Luz** is the process of **Ascension**.
